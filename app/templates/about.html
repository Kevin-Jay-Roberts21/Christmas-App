About

Christmas App Create your Christmas list, form groups with friends and family, and coordinate presents without spoilers!

  • My Lists: Create and edit your own Christmas lists.
  • My Groups: Create or join groups, and browse the lists of group members.
  • My Account: See your lists, group, and account info.
Frequently Asked Questions
  • Why can't I join a group?: To join a group, you must first create a list. Don't worry! The list doesn't have to be ready when you join a group, you can add items to your list later and everyone in your group will see the additions.
  • Can I add the same list to two different groups?: Yes! You can add the same list to as many groups as you want. All you edits to your list will be updated to each group!
  • Can I update my list?: Yes! You can remove or add items to your list and it will be updated in your group.
  • Can I add an item to someone else's list?: Yes! The item you add will NOT be seen by the recipient, but it will be seen by everyone else in the group.
  • If someone is in my group and another group, will the other group see my addition?: No! Additions to list remain within the group.
